# Health Services Act, No. 12 of 1952 - Meeting Information Research

**Research Date:** February 9, 2026  
**Ministry:** Ministry of Health  
**Act Status:** Active but largely superseded by Provincial Councils Act (1987/1989)

---

## Executive Summary

The Health Services Act of 1952 established three types of bodies:
1. **Health Council** - Still legally mandated, current operational status unclear
2. **Regional Hospitals Boards** - OBSOLETE (replaced 1989)
3. **Hospital Committees** - OBSOLETE (replaced 1989)

**Key Historical Change:** The 1989 Provincial Councils Act devolved health services, replacing Regional Hospitals Boards with **26 Regional Directorates of Health Services (RDHS)** under 9 Provincial Ministries.

## 1. Health Council (Section 4)

### Current Status
✅ **Legally Active** (legislation not repealed)  
❓ **Operationally Unknown** (no recent meeting records found)

### Composition
- **Total Members:** 11-12
- **Ex-Officio (4):**
  - Director of Health Services (Chair, non-voting)
  - Deputy Director (Medical Services)
  - Deputy Director (Public Health Services)
  - Deputy Director (Laboratory Services)
  
- **Nominated by Minister (7-8):**
  - Up to 7 Medical Practitioners (3 from medical, 3 from surgical, 3 from obstetrical sections)
  - 1 Dentist (from dental section)
  - Term: 2 years, eligible for re-nomination

### Meeting Requirements

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| **Frequency** | At least once per month |
| **Convened by** | Director of Health Services |
| **Chairperson** | Director (or member elected if Director absent) |
| **Quorum** | Not specified in available text |
| **Location** | Not specified |
| **Voting** | Director has no voting rights; other members vote |

### Functions
1. Make recommendations for improvement of health services
2. Tender advice to Minister on referred matters

### Reporting
- **To:** Secretary to the Ministry
- **When:** After each meeting
- **Route:** Through Director of Health Services
- **Dissent:** Members can record written dissent with reasons

### Data Gaps
- ❌ Current meeting schedules unknown
- ❌ Current membership list not found
- ❌ No recent meeting minutes or reports available online
- ❌ Actual meeting location not specified

## Current Health Governance Structure (2024)

### National Level
**Ministry of Health**
- Policy formulation
- Technical guidance
- Human resources training
- Drug procurement and distribution
- Disease control programs
- Management of 47 line ministry hospitals (including all teaching hospitals)

### Provincial Level
**9 Provincial Ministries of Health** (established 1989)
- **Leadership:**
  - Technical: Provincial Director of Health
  - Administrative: Provincial Secretary of Health
- **Responsibilities:**
  - Primary and secondary care
  - Preventive services
  - Hospital operations (District General, Base, Divisional)

### Regional Level
**26 Regional Directorates of Health Services (RDHS)**
- Implement provincial health plans
- Subdivided into 354 MOH areas
